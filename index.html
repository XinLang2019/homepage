About Me

Hi there! I am a Researcher Assistant in Controls and Robotics at Westlake University, working with Wang donglin. Before that, I worked on robotics research at SWUST. I obtained my bachelor from SWUST in 2022.

My goal is to create robust, intelligent, and agile robots for better human interaction. My current research focuses on the control and planning of legged robots using a combination of model-based optimal control and model-free reinforcement learning. I am also interested in expanding the capabilities of legged robots beyond locomotion.

I am always happy to collaborate with researchers from diverse backgrounds. If you are interested in my work, feel free to reach out!

Graduation Project

My undergraduate graduation project is to develop a four-legged robot, covering structural design, circuit design, motion algorithms, and system integration. This full-stack project took one year to complete.

1. Structural Design

Designed the mechanical structure using CAD software, ensuring lightweight and high-strength properties.

2. Circuit Design

Developed a custom PCB and integrated motor controllers, sensors, and communication modules.

3. Motion Algorithm

Implemented and optimized locomotion algorithms using reinforcement learning and model predictive control.

4. System Integration

Integrated software and hardware components, ensuring seamless communication and control.
